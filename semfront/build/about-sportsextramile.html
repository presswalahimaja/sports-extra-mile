About us

What is SportsExtraMile?

SportsExtraMile is a Sports social network with an aim to bring everybody associated with Sports on one platform.

We are building SportsExtraMile to encourage you to be more active, healthier & connected by promoting activities, facilities & discussions.

We truly believe, Sports is rejuvenating and fitness is necessary – everybody should be associated with at least one sport.

What do I do with SportsExtraMile?

You can discuss about any sports and create any sports related event. You can search for players and facilities around your location and grow your network.

As a sports enthusiast, you may want to build your profile around sports and let your friends know about it.

As a first timer, you may want to start with any Sports and know about it.

We want you to grow your passion for Sports.

Who can see my updates?

Your friends.

We are building SportsExtraMile in such way that you get your location specific information about the sports you are following.

How did the idea come about?

Ah, the story is based on our struggle to avail Badminton, Cricket and Table Tennis related information in our city. We explored available options and interacted with friends across cities – they had the same struggle.

Hence was the genesis of SportsExtraMile – perhaps its little long name in today’s funky world, but it conveys the objective clearly.
